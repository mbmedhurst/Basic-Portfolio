About Me

I was born and raised in Los Angeles. As an adult I’ve lived in San Diego, the UK (Oxford and London) and currently I’m living in Orange County, CA.

I have a Masters degree in English Literature, which I loved studying in college. But my career took me in a different direction. For the last 25 years I’ve worked in business systems development, including technical support, training, writing user guides, business analysis, user acceptance and usability testing, and now as a developer.

In my life away from my computer I enjoy traveling, cooking, watching Dodger baseball and paddle boarding with my dog.
